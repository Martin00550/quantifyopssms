Why Reviews Matter for Local Business

Online reviews significantly impact local search rankings and customer decisions. According to the Whitespark Local Search Ranking Factors study, review signals account for approximately 20% of local pack rankings, and high numerical ratings (4-5 stars) are among the most influential factors. Businesses with more positive reviews tend to appear higher in Google Maps results, which can lead to increased visibility.
